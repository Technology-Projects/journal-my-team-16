# Industry Cases: Legal, Ethical & Social Issues

Welcome! This repository contains your group’s template for analysing one real-world case and producing a concise, well‑evidenced recommendation.

## 🎯 Your task (summary)
- Read the assigned case in `cases/CASE-X.md`.
- Work in your group of three, rotating roles halfway through (Research Lead, Discussion Lead, Scribe & Synthesiser).
- Complete **all sections** in `REPORT.md`.
- Prepare a **2‑minute presentation** using `docs/presentation-template.md` (optional if your lecturer requires it).

## ⏱️ Suggested flow
1. **Case assignment** → open your case file in `cases/`.
2. **Individual quick scan** (5–7 min): note legal, ethical, social issues.
3. **Targeted search** (15–20 min): laws/regulations (e.g., GDPR, Equality Act, safety standards), ACM/IEEE codes, similar cases, academic/news sources.
4. **Deep discussion** (15–20 min): stakeholders, harms/benefits, legal clarity vs. ethical tensions, wider social consequences.
5. **Recommendation** (10–15 min): 4–6 bullet points with justification in `REPORT.md`.
6. **2‑minute presentation**: case summary → key issues → recommendations.

## 📦 What to submit
- A **completed `report.md`** with:
  - Clear stakeholder analysis
  - Evidence‑based legal and ethical reasoning (with citations/links)
  - 4–6 justified recommendations
  - Short reflection on collaboration


## ✅ Rubric (what we look for)
- **Depth of research**
- **Clarity of analysis**
- **Evidence of discussion**
- **Strength of recommendation**
- **Collaboration**

Good luck, and be concise and specific!
